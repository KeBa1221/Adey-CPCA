Safeguarding & Policies

Our commitment to child protection, financial integrity, and organizational accountability.

Our Commitment to Safety & Transparency

Adey CPCA operates with a zero-tolerance policy for any form of abuse, exploitation, or financial misconduct. All staff, volunteers, and partners are required to adhere to our comprehensive policies.

Our Policies

Policy Summaries

Children Safeguarding Policy

Child protection, abuse prevention, and reporting procedures

Child Protection Principles

  • Zero-tolerance for abuse, neglect, or exploitation
  • Best-interest-of-the-child in all decisions
  • Privacy and dignity of all children protected

Staff & Volunteer Requirements

  • Mandatory safeguarding orientation
  • Background screening for all personnel
  • Signed Code of Conduct required

Reporting & Response

Any concern should be reported to the Designated Safeguarding Lead immediately. All reports are treated confidentially. We cooperate fully with authorities in case of suspected abuse.

Finance Policy

Financial controls, audit requirements, and expenditure authorization

Financial Controls

  • Dual authorization for all significant expenditures
  • Monthly financial reporting to board
  • Segregation of duties for cash handling

Audit & Reporting

  • Annual independent financial audit
  • Donor funds tracked by project/purpose
  • Annual report published on request

Code of Conduct

Expected behavior and professional standards for all personnel

All Adey CPCA staff, volunteers, and contractors must adhere to the following standards:

  • Treat all children, families, and colleagues with respect and dignity
  • Never use discriminatory, offensive, or abusive language or behavior
  • Maintain strict confidentiality of beneficiary information
  • Never engage in or condone any physical, emotional, or sexual abuse
  • Report all concerns or violations to leadership immediately
  • Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose potential conflicts proactively

Whistleblower & Reporting

How to report concerns confidentially
