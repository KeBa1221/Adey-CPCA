About Us

Who is Adey CPCA?

Our Vision

An inclusive society where every child with cerebral palsy in Ethiopia receives the care, education, and opportunities they deserve — enabling dignified, fulfilling lives.

Our Mission

  • Raise awareness about CP in Ethiopian communities
  • Improve healthcare access and early intervention
  • Empower families and caregivers with resources
  • Promote inclusive education, advocacy, and research
Meet Our Team
Our Programs

What We Do

Healthcare & Therapy

Physiotherapy, occupational therapy, and early intervention programs for children with CP.

Inclusive Education

Advocating for inclusive classrooms and providing educational support materials.

Advocacy & Awareness

Community campaigns to reduce stigma and build disability-inclusive communities.

Assistive Devices

Sourcing and distributing wheelchairs, walkers, splints, and specialized seating systems.
